Introduction

Thank you for using our AntKeeper application ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Our Application only collects your email address when you create an account. This information is necessary for:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Sending you important notifications about your account
  • Contacting you regarding your use of the Application

We do not collect other personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, or any other information that could personally identify you.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect certain non-personal information, such as:

  • The type of mobile device you use
  • Your operating system version
  • Diagnostic information in case of application crashes

This information is collected solely to improve the Application's functionality and is not linked to your identity.

Stored Data

All data generated by your use of the Application, such as:

  • Your ant colonies
  • Your observations and notes
  • Your breeding statistics
  • Your application preferences
  • Your events and reminders

is stored securely in our database. This data is associated with your account and is only accessible to you. We use appropriate security measures to protect this information.

Use of Information

The collected information is used only for:

  • Maintaining and improving our Application
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Managing your account and contacting you when necessary

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, exchange, or transfer your information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our Application may use third-party services that collect information used to identify your device. These third-party providers have their own privacy policies regarding how they use this information.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.

Your Rights

Depending on your place of residence, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your information. Since our Application only collects your email address, these rights are automatically respected.

GDPR Compliance

Our Application is designed to comply with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). As we only collect your email address, the main GDPR requirements regarding consent, right to erasure, and data portability are naturally satisfied.
